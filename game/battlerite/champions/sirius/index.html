Sirius

Sirius 'the zenith' was born under a purple sky and wrapped in the light of the brightest star, the prophecy was true and foretold the birth of the Zenith. Taken from his parents and sent to a monastery to learn the the way of the astronomers, Sirius is a calm prodigy who uses the energies of stars and planets as destructive spells and healing powers.

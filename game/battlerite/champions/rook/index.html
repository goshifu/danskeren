Rook

Rook 'the hungering berserker' is a mighty chieftain rushing into battle with a giant hammer in one hand and his dinner in the other. Rook likes to smash his enemies, with boulders, with hammers and even with his body. Once he goes berserk he turns into an unstoppable force.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Pummel

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.35s
  • weapon charges: 3
  • type: melee

Strike in melee to deal damage. Successful hits reduces the cooldown of Crushing Blow.

Crushing Blow

RMB
  • cooldown: 7s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • air time: 0.5s
  • type: cleave

Jump into the air and slam your mace into the ground. Deals damage and inflicts Armor Break to enemies in the area, making the enemy take more damage.

Immaterial while airborne. Triggers counters and directional shields.

Rush

  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.7s
  • stun duration: 0.5s
  • type: mobility

Rush forward and slam into an enemy. Deals damage and inflicts Stun. Damage increases with travel distance.

Berserk

Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1s
  • invulnerability duration: 1s
  • berserk duration: 3s
  • haste percent: 50%
  • type: counter

Counter the next melee or projectile attack. Enter a berserk rage when you successfully counter an attack increasing your attack speed, movement speed and making you temporarily invulnerable.

Smack

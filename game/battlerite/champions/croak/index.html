Croak

Croak 'the ranid assassin' has a background shrouded in mystery, his movements are supernatural and his reputation is whispered about. Croak loves to surprise his enemies, using his mobility to move in and out. His chameleonic stealth and flexible fighting style makes him exceptionally hard to catch.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Blade Flurry

LMB
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 4%
  • cast time: 0.25s
  • recharge duration: 3s
  • type: melee

Strike with your blades to deal damage. Frog Leap and Camouflage increase the attack speed for a limited amount of strikes.

Blade Flurry has 4 weapon charges. Each charge causes Croak to attack faster.

Toxin Muck

RMB
  • cooldown: 6s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • air time: 0.8s
  • type: aoe

Spit toxin muck at target location. Deals damage, inflicts Toxin healing you over time.

Frog Leap

Space
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • recast duration: 1.8s
  • type: mobility

Leap attack and strike with your blades to deal damage in front of you. Can be recast.

Immaterial during the animation.

Camouflage

Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 3.5s
  • stun duration: 1s
  • fading haste: movement speed is increased by 30% and then fades over 2.5s.
  • type: cloak

Turn invisible and gain Fading Haste. Blade Flurry attacks made from Camouflage inflict Stun. Camouflage fades when an ability is used. Removes negative effects.

Can move through other players while in Camouflage. Camouflage resets Croak's weapon charges. A Blade Flurry that inflicts Stun will deal damage.

Noxious Lunge

E
  • cooldown: 9s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 4%
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • type: mobility

Lunge forward, piercing through enemies dealing damage.

Toxin Blades

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 4s
  • type: self buff

When activated your Blade Flurry attacks deal bonus damage, heal you and inflict Toxin.

EX(tra) Abilities

Sludge Spit

Shift + RMB
  • cooldown: 6s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • blind duration: 4s
  • type: projectile

Projectile attack the deals 10 damage, inflicts Toxin and reduces sight range by 80%.

Deceit

Shift + Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 2.5s
  • incapacitate duration: 3s
  • fading haste: movement speed is increased by 30% and then fades over 2.5s.
  • type: cloak

Turn invisible and gain Fading Haste. Blade Flurry attacks made from Deceit inflict Incapacitate and cause you to enter Camouflage. Deceit fades when an ability is used. Removes negative effects.

Can move through other players while in Deceit. A Blade Flurry that inflicts Incapacitate from Deceit will not deal any damage.

Ultimate Ability

Venom Wind

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • type: aoe, mobility

Dash forward in the shape of a venomous wind, piercing through enemies inflicting Venom, dealing massive damage after a duration.

Immaterial during the dash.

Debuffs

Toxin

Deals 8 damage to the target and heals self for 8 health over 5s.

Ticks after 1.25s, 2.5s, 3.75s and 5s. Cannot kill.

Venom

Deals 18 damage over 3s. Explodes when the effect ends, dealing 38 damage to nearby enemies.

Ticks on hit and after 1.5s and 3s. Cannot be cleansed by spells who remove negative effects.

Oldur

Oldur 'the time mender' is a mysterious creature able to bend time and space. He can mend wounds with powerful heals and reverse enemy attacks by freezing them in time.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Sands of Time

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.4s
  • type: projectile

Launch a projectile that deals damage and inflicts Sands of Time. Target takes additional damage after a duration.

Sands of Time debuff does not trigger on the Energy Rune.

Rejuvenating Sand

RMB
  • cooldown: 5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.5s
  • max charges: 3
  • cooldown reduction per ally: 1.5s
  • type: heal

Throw rejuvenating sand that heals the nearest ally. Recharges faster for each nearby ally.

Does not travel through terrain.

Shifting Sands

Space
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • type: mobility

Turn into sand and travel in target direction. Allies passed through are healed and enemies are dealt damage.

Immaterial during the animation. Travels through terrain. Oldur will not pick up health and energy orbs if he passes through them with Shifting Sands.

Time Bender

Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1.2s
  • recast duration: 4.5s
  • type: block

Negate projectile and melee attacks in front of you. Enemy melee attackers are knocked back and the first negated projectile will linger on within your hourglass, which you can launch by recasting this ability.

Quicksand

E
  • cooldown: 7s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • initial snare amount: 100%
  • fading snare duration: 1.5s
  • type: aoe

Throw an Hourglass to target location that explodes, dealing damage and inflicting Fading Snare. Consumes Sands of Time, dealing all damage instantly.

Stone Glass

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.5s
  • petrify duration: 3s
  • petrify break limit: 20 damage
  • type: cleave

Area attack that inflicts Stone Glass and knocks the target back.

Despite being a Melee attack, Stone Glass affects all enemies within the AoE.

EX(tra) Abilities

Chrono Bolt

Shift + LMB
  • cooldown: 0.2s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.5s
  • type: projectile

Launch a projectile that deals 16 damage and knocks target back. Consumes Sands of Time, dealing all damage instantly.

Chronobolt travels quicker and has significantly more range than Sands of Time.

Chronoflux

Shift + Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 3s
  • type: aoe, reflect

Place a time rift at target location. The rift reduces movement speed and reflects projectiles.

Slightly knocks back all units from the area of effect.

Ultimate Ability

Time Travel

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • impact delay: 2s
  • type: mobility

Target ally's cooldowns are accelerated. Target is removed from the fight for a short duration before reappearing at target location, inflicting Stun on all nearby enemies.

Debuffs

Sands of Time

Target takes 8 additional damage after 3.5s.

Stone Glass

Inflicts Petrify after 1s.

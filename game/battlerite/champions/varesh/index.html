Varesh

Varesh 'the eternal' is a being formed by the energies of others. Varesh uses his power to weaken and disrupt his enemies. By combining his powers he is able to unleash devastating attacks.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Hand of Corruption

LMB
  • cooldown: 0.5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 8%
  • cast time: 0.35s
  • type: projectile

Fire a projectile that deals damage, heals self for a small amount of health and inflicts Corruption.

Hand of Judgement

RMB
  • cooldown: 1.5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 10%
  • cast time: 0.9s
  • type: projectile

Fire a projectile that deals damage and inflicts Judgement.

Devastation battlerite grants 2.5% energy when hits.

Inhibitors Guard

Space
  • cooldown: 11s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • shield duration: 2.5s
  • type: shield

Shield a target ally. Shield absorbs damage.

Wuju

Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1.2s
  • air time: 0.8s
  • type: counter

Counter the next melee or projectile attack. Countering an attack teleports you to a new location and knocks away enemies. Consumes Judgement to deal bonus damage and to Silence the target. Consumes Corruption to heal you and to snare the target.

Shatter

E
  • cooldown: 5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • fading snare duration: 1s
  • silence duration: 0.5s
  • impact delay: 0.7s
  • type: aoe

Spawn an exploding shard at target location dealing area damage. Consumes Corruption to heal you and to snare the target. Consumes Judgement to deal bonus damage and to silence the target.

Reform

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • type: mobility

Dash forward in a target direction and spawn a Shatter shard at each enemy you pass through.

EX(tra) Abilities

  • cooldown: 1.5s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.4s

  • type: projectile

Crush

Shift + E
  • cooldown: 5s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • impact delay: 0.7s
  • type: aoe

Ultimate Ability

Powers Combined

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.8s
  • type: aoe, mobility

Dash forward in target direction dealing damage and inflicting both Judgement and Corruption on all enemies hit. This effect repeats itself up to 3 times as long as an enemy is hit.

Buffs

Debuffs

Corruption

Judgement

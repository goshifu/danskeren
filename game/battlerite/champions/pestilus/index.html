Pestilius

Pestilus 'the lord of the swarm' is the head priest of Quna. He serves the Qunan royal family, but secretly worships an evil deity, Rasal. His schemes manipulated the king into exiling the crown prince. In the arena, Pestilus unleashes swarms of insects to infest and cripple his enemies, releasing his innermost darkest desires.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Moth

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.25s
  • type: projectile

Projectile Attack that deals damage on enemy hit and heals health on ally hit. Inflicts Moth and heals you. Damage and healing done by Moth heal you over time.

Bloodsucker

RMB
  • cooldown: 4s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 10%
  • cast time: 0.6s
  • type: projectile

Projectile attack that deals damage on enemy hit, heals on ally hit and deals damage to self. Cannot deal lethal damage to self. Hitting a Queen with Bloodsucker kills it, without dealing any damage to self.

Self damage is only applied if Bloodsucker connects. Missing will not cause a reduction in health. Slightly knocks back target.

Infest

Space
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 10%
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • type: mobility

Lunge at enemies or allies to Infest them. Heals allies and self. Infested enemies take more damage and infested allies absorb some damage. Can be recasted to cancel the effect earlier.

Queen

Q
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • queen health: 30
  • type: aoe, summon

Summon a Queen that heals nearby allies and heals additional health over time. The Queen explodes after the duration, dealing damage to nearby enemies. This effect does not occur if the Queen is killed by an enemy.

Arachnophobia

E
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.4s
  • panic duration: 1.5s
  • impact delay: 1s
  • type: aoe

Throw a spider egg that breaks on impact inflicting Panic on all nearby enemies.

Swarm

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 2.5s
  • type: shield

Shield a target ally. Swarm absorbing damage and spreads Moth to nearby enemies if destroyed by damage.

EX(tra) Abilities

Vomit

Shift + RMB
  • cooldown: 4s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • root duration: 1.5s
  • type: cleave

Vomit upon enemies in front of you, dealing 8 damage and knocking enemies back. Inflicts Moth and Root.

Brain Bug

Shift + E
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.7s
  • type: projectile

Throw a Brain Bug that infests the enemy allowing you to control it's movement. Recasting this ability forces the target to run to the target location at 60% increased speed.

If reflected, the enemy who reflected Brain Bug cannot control Pestilus. Pestilus will instead be unable to move or cast spells.

Ultimate Ability

Scarab Pack

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.6s
  • duration: 7s
  • type: projectile

Summon and command 3 Scarabs to attack enemies or aid allies. A Scarab deals damage on enemy hit and heals health on ally hit. Scarabs stick to their target for a short duration. Damage and healing done by Scarabs heal self.

Slightly knocks back target.

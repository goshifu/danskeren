Poloma

Poloma 'the pyschopomp' is a psychopomp from the deep forests of Silverridge, Poloma uses shamanic powers to guide life and spirits alongside her protector and loyal creature, Toki. A dark disturbance among souls has put Poloma on a new path, leaving her safety and home behind. Driven by her burning curiosity and extraordinary senses, Poloma now seeks her answers in the arena.

Basic Abilities

Soul Bolt

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • soul bind damage: 20
  • soul bind duration: 6s
  • spirit link duration: 6s
  • type: projectile

Fire a Soul Bolt that deals damage and inflicts Soul Bind on enemy hit. Heals health and applies Spirit Link on ally hit.

Shimmering Bond does not work if the target healed is full health. Heals for 4 with Spirit Link if the target is full health.

Other Side

RMB
  • cooldown: 12s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1.8s
  • type: mobility

Send target ally into the spirit realm making them invulnerable and increases their movement speed. Heals self and nearby allies for health when the effect ends.

Heals the others 12 when your ally is linked at max health regardless of Shimmering Bond, 18 with Spirit Link (damaged ally), 20 with Shimmering Bond (damaged ally) Will not start until the target finishes channeling (if channeling). Can be casted on allies who are not in line of sight. Can be cancelled by the target to end it prematurely.

Spirit Guide

EX(tra) Abilities

Soul Transfer

Shift + Space
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • recast duration: 3s
  • type: projectile

Summon a spirit that does 16 damage to enemies or heals 20 health to allies. You and the target swap locations on impact. Can be recast again to teleport to the spirit if no target is hit.

If the Soul Transfer is successful, Poloma and the target will swap places. Damage and healing occurs when both characters meet at the midpoint of the transfer.

Soul Drain

Shift + Q
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • interrupt duration: 0.5s
  • panic damage limit: 16
  • type: aoe

Opens a rift that drains 15 health and interrupts the next ability cast by nearby enemies. Inflicts a 1.2s Panic when an interrupt is triggered. The panicked target runs at 15% increased movement speed.

Will not heal Spirit Linked ally if Poloma is full health.

Ultimate Ability

Ancestral Spirit

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • fading snare duration: 3s
  • type: aoe

Summon an Ancestral Spirit at target location. Deals damage and inflicts Fading Snare to enemies and heals allies. The spirit releases a second wave of energy after a short duration that deals more damage and heals more health.

Grants Poloma Immaterial for 1s upon cast. Removes debuffs on self upon cast. The initial hit occurs after a short delay.

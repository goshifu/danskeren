Raigon

Raigon 'the exiled prince' is the former crown prince of Quna. He was a well-respected figure among the kingdom’s warriors, until he was exiled due to the manipulations of Pestilus. For now, the arena serves as a decent place to find work and set the wheels in motion for his retaliation.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Sword Slash

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.35s
  • max charges: 3
  • type: melee

Strike with your sword to deal damage. Successful hits charges your weapon and reduces the cooldown of Retribution. Each charge increases the power of Retribution.

Retribution

RMB
  • cooldown: 5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • damage reduction per hit: 35%
  • healing reduction per hit: 35%
  • type: melee

Dash forward and strike to deal damage and leech health. Deals bonus damage and leeches bonus health per weapon charge.

Heavenly Strike

Space
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • air time: 0.55s
  • type: mobility

Leap and strike with your sword to deal damage in front of you upon landing.

Parry

Q
  • cooldown: 9s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • type: counter

Deflect up to 5 attacks. Melee attacks trigger a Counterattack and projectiles are redirected towards your aim direction.

Seismic Shock

E
  • cooldown: 9s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.8s
  • type: melee

Sunder the earth with your blade in a forward slash, dealing damage and launching struck enemy up in the air for a short duration.

Dragon Palm

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • fading snare duration: 0% over 1s
  • type: aoe

Dash forward and palm strike an enemy. Deals damage and knocks the enemy back, inflicting a Fading Snare. Knocking an enemy into a wall inflicts additional damage.

EX(tra) Abilities

Slicing Wind

Shift + LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.5s
  • max charges: 3
  • type: melee

Infuse your blade with determination, causing your next 2 Sword Slashes to send forth Slicing Winds. Deals 10 damage to enemies and heals you for 14 health.

Tectonic Shock

Shift + E
  • cooldown: 9s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.6s
  • type: aoe

Cleave the earth with your blade in a forward slash, dealing 10 damage and launching struck enemy up in the air for 1s. Inflicts Incapacitate for 2s upon landing.

Ultimate Ability

Wrath of the Tiger

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.8s
  • type: aoe

Dash and strike an enemy, dealing damage. Upon hit, slash all enemies in an area around you, dealing damage over time. When the duration ends or upon recast, dash towards your aim direction.

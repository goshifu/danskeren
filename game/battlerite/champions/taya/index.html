Taya

Taya 'the wind of the west' is an exceptionally swift hunter wielding a razor sharp boomerang. By shaping the wind around her taya is able to increase her speed and control her boomerang with deadly precision.

  • cast time: 0.3s
  • damage reduction per hit: 35%

Shift + Q

  • energy cost: 25

Ultimate Ability

Companion Call

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 5s
  • shield value: 38
  • razer boomerang damage: 15
  • type: mobility, summon

Your mount rushes towards you from target location dealing damage to enemies in its path. You are granted a shield and instantly mount when it reaches you. As long as the shield holds you cannot be demounted and you can throw empowered Razor Boomerangs.

If you are affected an ally Poloma's Other Side, and you cancel it while Companion Call is active, you will cancel both Other Side and Companion Call.

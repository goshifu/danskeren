Pearl

Pearl 'the ocean sage' is a sage able to control the oceans and whatever creatures that lurk below. Pearl transforms water into volatile attacks, rejuvenating streams and protecting bubbles.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Volatile Water

LMB
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.4s
  • silence duration: 1s
  • max charges: 1
  • type: projectile

Launch a projectile that deals damage. Deals damage and silences the enemy if your staff is fully charged.

Healing Wave

RMB
  • cooldown: 5s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.4s
  • cooldown reduction per ally: 1.5s
  • type: heal

Channel a healing wave that heals the nearest ally for. Recharges faster for each nearby ally.

Does not travel through terrain.

Dive

Space
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • type: mobility

Dive into a pool of water and travel to target location. Recharges your staff.

Tidal Wave

Q
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 7%
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • counter duration: 1.2s
  • type: counter

Counter the next melee or projectile attack causing a tidal wave that deals damage and knocks nearby enemies back. The wave also heals you.

Bubble Barrier

  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 0
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • duration: 4s
  • type: aoe

Spawn a bubble barrier at target location that slows enemy projectiles and enemies that enter the space.

Unstable Bubble

Shift + E
  • cooldown: 10s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • duration: 1.5s
  • type: aoe

Spawn a bubble barrier at target location that slows enemy projectiles and anyone that enters the space. Explodes when the effect ends dealing 26 damage and knocking enemies back.

Can be casted on the recast after a Bubble Barrier with Ocean Sage.

Ultimate Ability

Jaws

F
  • cooldown: 0s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 3.4s
  • recast duration: 3s
  • impact delay: 1s

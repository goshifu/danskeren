Jumong

Jumong 'the beast hunter' is a trophy collector who has wandered the wildlands in the pursuit of a worthy challenge. No longer content with hunting the great beasts of the world, has led him to enter the arena looking for a new type of prey. He traps his foes and ends them with a well placed shot from his mighty bow.

Skills

Basic Abilities

Hunting Arrow

LMB
  • cooldown: 0.25s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 9%
  • cast time: 0.45s
  • weapon charges: 3
  • type: projectile

Fire an arrow that deals damage. Successful hits charges your bow. A fully charged bow enables you to recast Steady Shot, Black Arrow, or Rain Of Arrows.

Steady Shot

RMB
  • cooldown: 6s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 12%
  • cast time: 1s
  • type: projectile

Projectile attack that deals damage.

Can consume Seeker's Mark.

Black Arrow

Space
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.2s
  • type: mobility

Dash towards move direction and fire an arrow that deals damage.

Immaterial during the animation.

Rain Of Arrows

Q
  • cooldown: 9s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 6%
  • cast time: 0.3s
  • duration: 2s
  • snare duration: 1s
  • snare value: 20%
  • type: aoe

Fire arrows that rain down at target location dealing damage over time and snaring enemies. The first hit inflicts Seeker's Mark.

Snare duration refreshes per tick of damage.

Bear Trap

E
  • cooldown: 8s
  • energy cost: 0
  • energy gain: 5%
  • cast time: 0.25s
  • duration: 6s
  • root duration: 2s
  • type: summon

Place a trap that inflicts Root on all nearby enemies when the trap is triggered.

Prowl

R
  • cooldown: 15s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1.2s
  • type: mobility

You become immaterial, increases your movement speed and all movement impairing effects are removed. Running into an enemy inflicts Seeker's Mark.

EX(tra) Abilities

Guided Arrow

Shift + LMB
  • cooldown: 12s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.1s
  • duration: 1s
  • type: projectile

Fire an arrow that bounces up to 3 times. Deals 6/6/6 damage, heals self for 6/6/6 health and inflicts Seeker's Mark on each hit.

Seekers Arrow

Shift + RMB
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 25
  • cast time: 0.6s
  • type: projectile

Fire a piercing arrow that turns to hit the same target again after impact dealing 12/12/12 damage. Each hit charges your weapon.

If the arrow projectile hits a wall while passing through an target, Seekers Arrow will end prematurely. The third hit of Seeker's Arrow benefits from the Steady Shot Battlerites. Only exception is that the knockback from Power Shot is not applied.

Ultimate Ability

Dragon Slayer

F
  • cooldown: 1s
  • energy cost: 100
  • cast time: 0.9s
  • channel duration: 3s
  • type: projectile

Hold to charge an arrow to increase its damage and travel distance. The arrow deals damage and pulls the target hit with it. Pulling a target into an enemy or into a wall deals damage to the enemy hit and stuns both targets.

Buffs

Prowl

You become temporarily immaterial and movement speed is increased by 100% for 1.2s.

Debuffs

Seeker's Mark

Hunting Arrow and Black Arrow consumes the mark healing you for 12 health. Mark lasts for 3s.
